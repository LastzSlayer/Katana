Glory Wars

Glory Wars are won by discipline, timing, target focus, and clean communication. Follow the standards below so the alliance moves as one unit.

⏱️

Be On Time

Arrive before war starts. Check assignments, buffs, marches, hospital space, and rally instructions.

🎯

Follow Targets

Do not random hit. Prioritize marked targets, rally calls, weak defenses, and leadership instructions.

📣

Use Discord

War calls, rally timing, target switches, and defensive alerts should be handled in Discord whenever possible.

Battle Expectations

  • Scout before major attacks unless leadership says otherwise.
  • Join rallies quickly with the requested troop type and march size.
  • Protect your troops, heal smart, and avoid feeding points.
  • Share enemy movements, traps, shields dropping, and rally targets.
  • Respect leadership calls even if plans change during battle.
